# Getting Started

Welcome to **KDAnalytiks**, your comprehensive platform for building questionnaires, deploying campaigns, collecting responses, and running advanced analysis.

## Overview

KDAnalytiks is designed to bridge the gap between survey creators and respondents. Whether you are a researcher, a corporate marketing team, or an academic, the platform provides tools that scale from basic questionnaire design to complex data synthesis.

### Key Navigation Hubs

1. **Dashboard**: The main home for managing your active projects, drafts, and overall statistics.
2. **Survey Builder**: The graphical editor for designing questions.
3. **Analytics Dashboard**: Real-time summaries, charts, cross-tabulation, and inferential analysis.
4. **Collaboration Section**: Add colleagues to collaborate on research or create isolated student groups.

### Platform Roles

- **Admins**: Manage users, tiers, and system-wide configurations.
- **Organizations**: Corporate profiles supporting multi-user cooperation and enterprise billing.
- **Independent Researchers**: Personal profiles tailored for individual academic or market research.
- **Respondents**: User accounts built to complete public or invited surveys, build wallets, and cash out survey rewards.
