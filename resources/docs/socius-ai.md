# Socius AI Assistant

**Socius** is your personal, AI-powered research assistant integrated directly inside the survey reports dashboard.

## What can Socius do?

- **Executive Summary**: Generates structured, high-level summaries of all responses instantly.
- **Granular Querying**: Ask questions like "Summarize the complaints from users aged 18-25" or "Synthesize the open-ended text answers."
- **Memory & Guidelines**: Set stylistic guidelines and guidelines (such as target formats, tone, or constraints) that Socius will remember across your analysis sessions.
- **Visual Synthesis**: Request Socius to generate custom charts, flowcharts, or images based on response statistics.

## Thread Management

- **Pinning**: Keep important threads pinned to the top of the history list for quick access.
- **Exporting**: Export entire AI conversation logs to clean HTML or PDF formats for external reports.
- **Privacy & Groups**: AI threads are private to individual researchers, or shared strictly within assigned **Analysis Groups**.
